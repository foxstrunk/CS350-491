Biography

I was diagnosed with Schizophrenia only a week after leaving for college. I firmly believe in being very open about my diagnosis, the way I see it, the world isn't going to change it's veiws on it's own. There is a lot of misinformation about mental illness in our society, and I believe that the best way to fix it, is to be the counter example. I have schizophrenia, I am not dangerous, and I will be successful in life.
